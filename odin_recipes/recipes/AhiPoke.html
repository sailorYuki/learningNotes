Ahi Poke

Description

This easy poke recipe is a refreshing Hawaiian salad of fresh ahi tuna steak cubes tossed with soy sauce, sesame oil, and green onions for a dish full of umami flavor. I like to add chopped macadamia nuts even though they are not authentic â€” they add a delicious crunch! This makes 4 main course servings or 8 appetizer servings.

Ingredients

  • Ahi Tuna
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Green Onions
  • Toasted Sesame Seeds
  • Macadamia Nuts
  • Crushed Red Pepper (Optional)

Directions

  1. Simply combine all the ingredients in a bowl and allow it to marinate in the fridge for at least two hours. Serve as desired.
